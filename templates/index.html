Understanding Breast Cancer

Breast cancer is a disease in which cells in the breast grow out of control. It is the most common cancer diagnosed in women worldwide. Early detection and awareness are crucial in improving outcomes.

This website aims to provide information, support, and resources related to breast cancer, detection, and support systems.
